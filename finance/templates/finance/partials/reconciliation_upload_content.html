How It Works

  1. Select your bank and upload the statement (CSV or Excel)
  2. The system matches deposits to invoices using invoice numbers and admission numbers
  3. Review matches and manually assign any unmatched rows
  4. Confirm to create payments in bulk

Tips

  • Best results when parents include the invoice number (e.g. INV-2026-00012) as payment reference
  • Admission numbers in the description are also matched automatically
  • Duplicate references (already recorded payments) are flagged
